What is GiftCrossing

Have you ever get a Gift that you don`t like or don`t want, or don`t need? If this was happened or happens time to time, don`t worry. You don`t have to let the Gift clutter your home andcollect dust. You can: sell it, donate it, reGift it, repurpose it or.. trade it in Giftcrossing.
GiftCrossing is the act of releasing your gifts "into the wild" for a stranger to find, or via "controlled release" to another GiftCrossing member, and tracking where they go via journal entries from around the world. We hope you join us!

How it works


Register your gift for FREE, and get a unique GiftCrossing ID. Do not forgen to create your own Wish list.

Find your Gift in an other member Wish list. Apply your Gift in a member Wish list. Receive an unique code and send the Gift.
